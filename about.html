Who We Are

Where tradition meets innovation, crafting exceptional coffee and pastries with passion and precision

Ex Espresso, a place where sophistication meets edge in every sip and bite. Nestled in the heart of Manchester, our cafe is more than just a place to grab your morning brew; it's an experience crafted with passion and dedication by our team of traditional bakers and baristas. With a shared love for the craft of bakery and a mission to keep the art of artisan coffee brewing alive, we're here to redefine your cafe experience. From our meticulously crafted pastries to our expertly brewed coffees, every detail is curated to delight your senses and elevate your day. Join us on a journey where tradition meets innovation, and indulge in the artistry of Ex Espresso.

How We Started

A shared passion for coffee and patisserie, nurtured and expanded through voyages across Africa and Europe

Our journey began in the rural town of Machakos, Kenya, where our team's roots stretch deep into the soil of tradition and craftsmanship. United by a shared passion for the alchemy of coffee and the artistry of baked treasures, we honed our skills amidst the lush landscapes and warm embraces of community. We embarked on voyages of discovery across Ethiopia, tracing the ancient paths along the River Nile and traversing the vibrant markets of North West Africa. Each journey expanded our horizons and enriched our understanding of the cultural tapestry woven through every cup and pastry. It was amidst the bustling streets and rich history of Europe that many of us found our final destination, settling in the vibrant enclave of Spinningfields, Manchester. Here, amidst the hustle and bustle of city life, our traditions found new roots, and Ex Espresso was born—a testament to our journey, our passion, and our unwavering commitment to crafting exceptional experiences one sip and one bite at a time.

What's Next

Dedicated to spreading the art of artisan coffee and pastry, we are sharing our passion for skill and craft with lovers of exceptional taste

As we embark on the next chapter of our journey, our mission at Ex Espresso is clear: to spread the rich story and exquisite craft of artisan coffee and pastry bakery across Modern Europe. With each new city we venture into, we bring to life our unwavering dedication to the lovers of skill and craft, creating spaces where tradition meets innovation, and every cup and pastry tells a tale of passion and precision. As we continue to expand our footprint, our goal remains steadfast—to inspire and delight, to educate and elevate, and to foster a community bound by a shared love for the artistry of coffee and baked treasures. Together, we look forward to writing the next chapter of our story, one city at a time, as we continue to spread the warmth and flavor of Ex Espresso throughout Europe and beyond.
