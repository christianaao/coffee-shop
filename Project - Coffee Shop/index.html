Extraordinary Coffee

Welcome to Ex Espresso, where extraordinary coffee begins with a journey across continents. We take pride in sourcing only the finest organic Arabica coffee beans, handpicked from the lush landscapes of Machakos, Kenya. From the moment our beans are harvested to the final pour into your cup, every step of the process is carefully curated to ensure a truly exceptional coffee experience. Join us on a journey of flavor and discovery as we bring the rich traditions and bold flavors of Kenya straight to your doorstep. Welcome to the world of Ex Espresso, where every cup tells a story.
